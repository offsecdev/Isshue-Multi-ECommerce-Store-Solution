Woo-commerce

New Add-Ons

If you want to connect your wordpress woo-commerece site with isshue then this module is for you. To buy, go to the "Module" module from side bar menu. Click on buy now then install. After buying and installing woo-commerece a new module will appear on the left-side bar with the other previous modules of isshue.



Woo-Commerce Setting

Now go to your wordpress admin panel. Go to woo-commerce > setting. click advanced > Rest API. Add description, user. Give permission for read/write . Now click generate API key. It will create a consumer key and consumer secret. Now go to isshue admin panel > woo-commerce setting. Enter your wordpress url, consumer key and consumer secret, select order sync system (manual/automatic) click the check box on if you want to sync the product from local to wordpress site, now submit.








Synchronization Proccess

To work with woocommerce, you need to sync all item from you local to woocommerce site. To sync items, follow the sequence-

  1. Category
  2. Attirbutes and Attribute Terms
  3. Products
  4. Product Variants
  5. Orders

First you have to sycn the Category then Attirbutes and Attirbutes Terms. After this 2 steps,you are ready to product sync. Now you can synchronize your store product with your woo-commerce product. Go to woo-commerce > product> local product. All the product list will appear here. If you click on the upload button in the action, it will directly upload to woo-commerce with it's local attribute and variants. Or you can go to edit and choose a woo-commerce product with which you want to synchronize, then click sync button. Now you can sync the orders




You can synchronize your woo-commerce product with your store product. Go to woo-commerce > product > woo product. All the product list will appear here. If you click on the download button in the action, it will directly download to woo product to local product. Or you can go to edit and choose a local product with which you want to synchronize, then click sync button.



N.B. If you change any type of any product after synchronization from woo-commerce then you need to update synchronize the product from local product to woo eCommerce again.

Product Purchase and Manage

During a product purchase if you want to sync the product instantly click on the stock check box. Also during editing any product purchase you can click stock check box if you want to sync it to woo-commerce.





order and Sales

Online orders will come to manage order module. To accept the order click on the yellow button in action. it will make the invoice automatically for that product. Now go to manage sale and change the status to complete. The product stock will only update if you change the status to complete otherwise there will be no change in stock.

Also for store product you have to do the same. After making POS invoice Go to manage sale and change the status to complete for updating the product stock.
